Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: February 2024

1. Introduction & Scope

Adeptskil Technologies ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data handling practices. This Privacy Policy governs how we collect, use, process, store, and protect your personal information when you access our website (https://adeptskil.com), enroll in training courses, interact with our platform, or communicate with our team.

Adeptskil provides professional training and corporate education services, offering 200+ courses across multiple disciplines including leadership development, soft skills training, project management, agile methodologies, IT security, cloud computing, data management, and business development.

2. Legal Basis & Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. Our processing of your data is based on:

  • Contractual Necessity: To provide training services and manage your enrollment
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services and maintain platform security
  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional data uses

3. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Registration & Enrollment Data: Full name, email address, phone number, company/organization, job title, professional background, and course preferences
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed through secure payment gateways; we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Profile Information: Educational qualifications, professional certifications, learning goals, and skill assessment results
  • Communication Data: Messages, support tickets, feedback, and survey responses
  • Course Performance Data: Progress tracking, test scores, assignments, and course completion certificates

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers, and timestamps
  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, time spent on each page, links clicked, course modules accessed, video watch duration, and interaction patterns
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session cookies, preference cookies, and analytics tools (Google Analytics)
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS)

C. Information from Third Parties

  • Corporate partner information about employee enrollment programs
  • Payment processor data for verification purposes
  • Third-party learning management system (LMS) integration data

4. How We Use Your Information

A. Primary Service Delivery

  • Course enrollment, access, and delivery
  • Sending course materials, lesson updates, and learning resources
  • Tracking progress and providing performance feedback
  • Issuing certificates of completion and digital badges
  • Account management and login authentication

B. Communications & Support

  • Responding to inquiries and providing customer support (24/7)
  • Sending important notifications about course changes, technical issues, or policy updates
  • Conducting surveys and collecting feedback to improve services
  • Handling refund requests and processing complaints

C. Marketing & Business Development

  • Sending promotional content, newsletters, and new course announcements (with your consent)
  • Recommending courses based on your interests and learning history
  • Conducting market research and analyzing user behavior trends
  • Creating anonymized performance reports for training effectiveness

D. Security & Legal Compliance

  • Preventing fraud, unauthorized access, and platform abuse
  • Verifying user identity and authentication
  • Complying with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Protecting intellectual property and enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Auditing and monitoring platform security

5. Data Retention

  • Active User Accounts: Retained for the duration of your relationship with us
  • Inactive Accounts: Retained for 2 years after last login, then deleted unless legal obligations require retention
  • Course Records & Certificates: Retained for 7 years for verification and legal compliance purposes
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years as per legal and financial audit requirements
  • Support Communications: Retained for 3 years or as required by law
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent
  • Cookies: Session cookies deleted upon browser close; persistent cookies retained for up to 2 years

6. Disclosure & Data Sharing

We May Share Your Information With:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, email platforms, analytics services, hosting providers, and LMS providers who process data on our behalf under Data Processing Agreements
  • Corporate Partners: When enrollment is through a corporate training program, we may share completion status with your employer
  • Affiliate Partners: Educational technology partners and course content providers (under confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Successors: In case of merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of assets

We DO NOT: Sell, trade, rent, or profit from your personal information. We never share personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes without explicit written consent.

7. Data Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Password hashing and salting with industry-standard algorithms
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Multi-factor authentication options for account access
  • Role-based access control for internal data access
  • Data encryption at rest in our databases
  • Secure firewall and intrusion detection systems
  • Regular backup and disaster recovery procedures
  • Employee data protection training and confidentiality agreements
  • Incident response procedures for potential data breaches

Note: While we employ industry best practices, no system is 100% secure. We urge you to use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

8. International Data Transfers

Adeptskil operates from India and our servers are located within India. If you are accessing our platform from outside India, your data will be transferred to and processed in India. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your data to India and acknowledge that data protection laws may differ.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations and contractual requirements)
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format for transfer to another service
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of marketing communications and certain data processing activities
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent for optional data processing at any time
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at: info@adeptskil.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our platform uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze usage patterns:

  • Essential Cookies: Enable login, session management, and platform functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Track usage patterns through Google Analytics to improve user experience
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your language, theme, and notification preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for retargeting ads (only with consent)

You can control cookies through your browser settings or opt-out of analytics tracking. Disabling essential cookies may impact platform functionality. See our Cookie Policy for detailed information.

11. Third-Party Links & Services

Our platform contains links to third-party websites (social media, payment processors, course partner sites). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies independently. Our disclosure of third-party links does not constitute endorsement.

12. Children's Privacy

Adeptskil is designed for professional learners, typically aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover we've collected data from a child under 13, we will immediately delete it and notify the parent/guardian. For users 13-18, we require parental consent for data processing.

13. Opt-Out & Communication Preferences

  • Email Marketing: Unsubscribe via the link in every marketing email or update your notification preferences in your account settings
  • SMS/Push Notifications: Manage preferences in your account dashboard
  • Cookies: Adjust browser cookie settings or use our cookie preference center

You may continue to receive essential service communications (course updates, support responses, payment confirmations) even if you opt-out of marketing.

14. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach involving your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours (where legally required)
  • Provide details about the nature of the breach
  • Describe measures taken to mitigate harm
  • Offer credit monitoring or identity protection services (if applicable)
  • Report to relevant data protection authorities as required by law

15. Contact & Privacy Requests

For all privacy-related inquiries, data subject requests, or to file a complaint:

Adeptskil Technologies
Email: info@adeptskil.com
Subject Line: "Privacy Request"
Support: 24/7 Available
Response Time: Within 30 days

16. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. Material changes will be notified via email or prominent notification on our website. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

17. EU & California Residents

GDPR (EU): If you are located in the EU, additional rights apply under GDPR. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

CCPA (California): California residents have additional rights including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale of personal information. Our Privacy Policy complies with CCPA requirements.
